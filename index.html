Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Extension: that cool widget

No data collected

that cool widget does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal or usage data. The extension operates entirely within your browser.

What is stored locally

The extension uses chrome.storage.local to save your preferences on your own device only — specifically the widget's position on screen, whether it is collapsed or expanded, and your chosen colour theme (light or dark). This data never leaves your device and is inaccessible to the developer or any third party.

Permissions

The extension requests access to YouTube Music tabs solely to send playback commands (play, pause, skip, seek) from the widget. No browsing history, page content, or personal information is read or recorded.

Third parties

No data is sold, rented, or transferred to any third party under any circumstances.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please open an issue on the extension's GitHub repository.
